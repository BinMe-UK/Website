Frequently asked questions

Which councils are supported?

BinMe currently covers Sevenoaks District Council. We're expanding — if your area isn't listed, keep an eye out on this page for when it is.

How do I sign up?

Text BINME to +44 7888 874879 from your mobile. We'll guide you through setup via SMS — it takes about two minutes. No app and no account to get started.

Which bin types are covered?

All standard household bin types: general waste, recycling, food waste, and garden waste. During setup you can choose which ones apply to your address.

When will I receive my reminder?

You choose — either the evening before your collection or the morning of. You can change this at any time by texting CHANGE to +44 7888 874879.

What happens on bank holidays?

When your council moves a collection due to a bank holiday, we send you an updated reminder with the revised date. You don't need to do anything — we track changes automatically.

How much does it cost?

£12 per year. Standard network message rates apply for texts you send to us.

How do I change my preferences?

Text CHANGE to +44 7888 874879 and we'll guide you through updating your postcode, bin types, and reminder timing.

How do I cancel?

Text STOP to +44 7888 874879 at any time. Your reminders stop immediately and your data is deleted. No questions asked.

Can I use BinMe for more than one address?

Each phone number is linked to one address. If you need reminders for multiple addresses, get in touch at help@binme.uk.

Something isn't working — how do I get help?

Email us at help@binme.uk and we'll sort it out.
